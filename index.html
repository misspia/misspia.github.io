DropChat Mobile App

TAGLINE
Communicate with friends, or strangers around you.
THE PROBLEM
Communicating with those around is always a difficult process to initiate, especially when there are a large number of strangers, or you are in an unfamilliar environment.
For example, when an event attendee or a store customer would like to ask a question, they would have to seek out and identify an organizer or an employee, then proceed with their question. What if there was a way to instantly connect with anyone who walks into a specific event or store?
THE IMPLEMENTATION
DropChat lets its users create chat groups based on their location. Users specify the radius of their chat by zooming in and out of the main map view; they set the name, number of people who may join, as well as an optional password. Anyone user who walks into this radius can see the contents of the chat.
If an event attendee or a store customer want to ask a question, they can pop open the event or store's chat, and ask away. Organizers/employees or anyone within the event/store can answer right away.
